Adam Gray

Educational Background
• Bachelor's degree in Political Science from the University of California, Santa Barbara

Career
• Elected member of the California State Assembly, representing District 21 (2012-2022) where he was on a number of committees and the chairman of the Governmental Organization Committee
• Worked in the district office of state assemblyman Dennis Cardoza; served as a staffer for three assembly members before joining the staff of state senator Ron Calderon
• Supports funding medical education and better access to healthcare, remembering veterans, and retaining natural resources like water and using renewable energy; does not support defunding general education

Personal Information
• Worked at his family’s dairy supply and feed store when he was young
• Teaches a class on the Legislature at UC Merced
• Was married to Cadee Condit; they have one child together
