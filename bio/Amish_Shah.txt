Amish Shah

Educational Background
• Got his Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Northwestern University
• Earned a Doctor of Medicine degree at Northwestern's Feinberg School of Medicine
• Completed a Master of Public Health degree from UC Berkeley School of Public Health

Career
• Became a faculty member and academic researcher at Mount Sinai Medical Center after completing residency in New York City
• Medical career continued with being a team physician for the New York Jets, chief medical officer of Urgent Consult, and an emergency physician at the Mayo Clinic Arizona in 2023
• Elected to the House of Representatives in 2018 for Arizona’s 24th District, later re-elected in 2020 and 2022; in the last race he won Arizona’s 5th District due to redistricting

Personal Information
• Amish enjoys playing basketball, flying small airplanes, and embarking on exciting travels
• Speaks conversational Spanish and is fluent in Gujarati
• He has three adopted cats named Hillary, Miss Meowerson, and Cousin Oliver
