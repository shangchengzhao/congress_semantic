Educational Background
Got his Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Northwestern University (Wikipedia)
Earned a Doctor of Medicine degree at Northwestern's Feinberg School of Medicine (Wikipedia)
Completed a Master of Public Health degree from UC Berkeley School of Public Health (Wikipedia)

Career
Became a faculty member and academic researcher at Mount Sinai Medical Center after completing residency in New York City (Wikipedia)
Medical career continued with being a team physician for the New York Jets, chief medical officer of Urgent Consult, and an emergency physician at the Mayo Clinic Arizona in 2023 (Wikipedia)
Elected to the House of Representatives in 2018 for Arizona’s 24th District, later re-elected in 2020 and 2022; in the last race he won Arizona’s 5th district due to redistricting (Wikipedia)
