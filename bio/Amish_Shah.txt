Amish Shah

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Economics from Northwestern University
• Doctor of Medicine degree at Northwestern's Feinberg School of Medicine
• Master of Public Health degree from UC Berkeley School of Public Health

Career
• Elected member of the Arizona House of Representatives, representing District 24 (2019-2023) and District 5 (2023)
• Was a faculty member and academic researcher at Mount Sinai Medical Center; later worked as a team physician for the New York Jets, chief medical officer of Urgent Consult, and emergency physician at the Mayo Clinic
• Supports universal healthcare and reproductive rights, increasing funding for teacher pay and educational materials, and voting rights; does not support gerrymandering and the influence of money in politics (/)

• Enjoys playing basketball, flying small airplanes, and embarking on exciting travels
