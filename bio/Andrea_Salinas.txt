Educational Background
Earned her Bachelor of Arts degree from University of California, Berkeley (Wikipedia)

Career
Federal lobbyist for National Treasury Employees Union for two years then moved to Portland and later lobbied for another two years (Wikipedia)
Legislative aids for U.S. Senator Harry Reid and U.S. Representatives Pete Stark and Darlene Hooley before being the legislative director of the Oregon Environmental Council and later starting her own legislative consulting practice  (Wikipedia)
VP of Strategy 360 political consulting firm before joining the Oregon House of Representatives in 2017 and later the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022  (Wikipedia)
