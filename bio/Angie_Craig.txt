Angie Craig

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Art degree in Journalism from the University of Memphis

Career
• Elected as U.S. Representative from Minnesota’s 2nd Congressional District (2019-2023)
• Previous Global Human Resources Leader at Saint Jude Medical (2005-2015) and current Director at Saint Jude’s Global Human resources Group (starting 2011)
• Supports government regulation of prescription drug costs, increasing security along the southern US border, and increasing regulations on social media companies; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade

Personal Information
• Granddaughter of a farm foreman
• Married to her wife, Cheryl; they have four sons and three grandsons
• Her mother was a single mom who raised three children while earning her teaching degree
