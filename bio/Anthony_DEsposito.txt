Anthony DEsposito

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Arts degree from Hofstra University in New York

Career
• Police officer for NYPD’s 73rd Precinct in Brooklyn, then joined the Precinct Detective Squad and Military and Extended Leave Desk, he worked there for 14 years
• Appointed as councilman on Hempstead, New York town council in 2016 and won a full term the year after
• Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022 for New York’s 4th District

Personal Information
• Anthony is of Italian and Puerto Rican descent
• He is a volunteer with the Oceanside United Soccer Club and awarded Volunteer of the Year in 2019-2020
