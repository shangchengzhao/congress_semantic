Educational Background
Bachelor of Arts degree from Hofstra University in New York (Wikipedia)

Career
Police officer for NYPD’s 73rd Precinct in Brooklyn, then joined the Precinct Detective Squad and Military and Extended Leave Desk, he worked there for 14 years (Wikipedia)
Appointed as councilman on Hempstead, New York town council in 2016 and won a full term the year after (Wikipedia)
Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022 for New York’s 4th District (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Anthony is of Italian and Puerto Rican descent (Wikipedia)
He is a volunteer with the Oceanside United Soccer Club and awarded Volunteer of the Year in 2019-2020 (D’Esposito Campaign Website)
