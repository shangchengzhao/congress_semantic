Austin Theriault

Educational Background
• Associate of Arts degree from Central Piedmont Community College
• Attended University of Maine at Presque Isle

Career
• Elected to serve in the Main House of Representatives for the 1st District (2022)
• Raced in competitions like NASCAR Cup Series and Arca while mentoring young drivers; took over his own career and managed all of his own negotiations, sponsorships, and administrative tasks at 16 years old
• Supports improving infrastructure like roads, introducing manufacturing jobs and lowering energy costs, and securing the border; does not support cutting medicare and social security

Personal Information
• Comes from a family of loggers and farmers who are all from Aroostook County, Maine
• Born and raised in Fort Kent, Maine
