Brandon Williams

Educational Background
• Started university at Baylor University
• Transferred to Pepperdine University where he completed a Bachelor of Arts degree in liberal arts, completing a year at Harvard College as a visiting undergraduate student
• Completed a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School of Management

Career
• Joined the navy in 1991 and served as a naval officer on the submarine US Georgia
• Business executive and investment banker, co-founding and investing in multiple companies including IgniteIP and CPLANE
• Co-founded the startup funding company IgniteIP and founded the industrial process software company CPLANE (CEO)

Personal Information
• He met his wife Stephanie at a Bible study group in Florida while he was in the navy
• He and his wife purchased a 67 acres farm in Sennett, New York in 2008, focusing on bees, lavender, and truffles
• Brandon has lived in Florida, Texas, New York City, Seattle, and Silicon Valley
