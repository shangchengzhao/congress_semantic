Educational Background
Started university at Baylor University (Wikipedia)
Transferred to Pepperdine University where he completed a Bachelor of Arts degree in liberal arts, completing a year at Harvard College as a visiting undergraduate student  (Wikipedia)
Completed a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School of Management (Wikipedia)

Career
Joined the navy in 1991 and served as a naval officer on the submarine US Georgia (Wikipedia)
Business executive and investment banker, co-founding and investing in multiple companies including IgniteIP and CPLANE (Wikipedia)
Co-founded the startup funding company IgniteIP and founded the industrial process software company CPLANE (CEO), wikipedia

Personal Information
He met his wife Stephanie at a Bible study group in Florida while he was in the navy (Wikipedia)
He and his wife purchased a 67 acres farm in Sennett, New York in 2008, focusing on bees, lavender, and truffles, wikipedia
Brandon has lived in Florida, Texas, New York City, Seattle, and Silicon Valley, wikipedia
