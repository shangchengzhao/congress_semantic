Chris DeLuzio

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree with merit from the United States Naval Academy
• Juris Doctor degree from Georgetown University Law Center

Career
• Elected to serve as the U.S. Representative for Pennsylvania’s 17th Congressional District (2023); was previously a litigation associate at a law firm and joined the Brennan Center of Justice to work on voting rights and election security
• Served as a naval officer where he was a surface warfare officer and deployed to Iraq with an Army civil affairs unit (2006-2012)
• Supports increasing federal funding for safety net and affordable housing programs and wealth taxes to pay for public programs; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade and requiring government ID to vote at polls

Personal Information
• Originally from Thornburg, Pennsylvania; father owns a healthcare management consulting firm
• Met his wife Alexandra while attending law school at Georgetown
• Played baseball while attending Bishop Canevin High School
