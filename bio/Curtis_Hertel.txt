Curtis Hertel

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Social Relations from James Madison College at Michigan State University

Career
• Worked for the Michigan House Democratic Caucus (2001-2013)
• Policy analyst for the Department of Community Health (2004-2005) and legislative liaison (2005-2008)
• Served on the Ingham County Board of commissioners (2001-2008); Ingham County Register of Deeds (2008-2014)

Personal Information
• His wife, Elizabeth, served as Michigan’s Department of Health and Human Services director
• He and his wife have four children together named CJ, Nathan, Hailey, and Jack
• Son of former Representative Curtis Hertel, Sr., who passed in 2016
