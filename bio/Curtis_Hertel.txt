Educational Background
Bachelor of arts degree from James Madison College at Michigan State University (Wikipedia)

Career
Ingham County Board of Commissioners for 7 years represented East Lansing and Meridian Township before being a Legislative Liaison for the Department of Community Health for Jennifer Granholm for 3 years (Wikipedia)
Elected to Ingham Country REgister of Deed and later reelected to a second term four years later (Wikipedia)
Elected to Michigan state senate in 2013 where he represented Michigan’s 23rd district, he was later reelected in 2018; he was ineligible for reelection in 2022 and joined Grethcne Whitmer’s administration as a top lobbyist (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Hertel is married to his wife Elizaberth who served as Michigan’s Department of Health and Human Service director (Wikipedia)
