Dan Newhouse

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science degree in agricultural economics from Washington State University
• Washington Agriculture and Forestry Leadership Program

Career
• Served four terms in the Washington House of Representatives (2003 - 2009)
• State Director of the Washington State Department of Agriculture (2009-2013)
• Joined the United States House of Representatives in 2014 and was reelected in 2015

Personal Information
• Owns a 600 acre farm in Sunnyside that produces a variety of produce
• His first wife Carol died of cancer in 2017; remarried to his second wife Joan Galvin in 2018 at the United States Capitol
• His parents and all his siblings also graduated from Washington State University
