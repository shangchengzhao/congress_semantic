David Schweikert

Educational Background
• High school degree from Saguaro High School
• Bachelor of Science degree in Finance and Real Estate from W.P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University
• Masters of Business Administration from W.P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University

Career
• Arizona State House of Representatives for District 28 (1990-1995)
• Chair of the Arizona State Board of Equalization (1995-2003); managed billions of dollars in valuations and tax protests from Arizona citizens and businesses
• Chief Deputy Treasurer of Maricopa County (2004-2007)

Personal Information
• Grew up in Scottsdale with his adoptive parents and two adopted siblings
• He and his wife, Joyce adopted a daughter in 2015 and a son in 2022
• He and his wife enjoy backpacking, spending time outdoors, and host overnight hikes down the Grand Canyon
