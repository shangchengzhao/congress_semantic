David Valadao

Educational Background
• Graduated from Hanford High School in California
• Attended the College of the Sequoias in Visalia as a part-time student, but did not graduate

Career
• Member of the California Milk Advisory Board and Western States Dairy Trade Association
• Regional Leadership Council chairman for Land O’Lakes
• Won the U.S. House of Representatives race for California's 21st Congressional District, re-elected in 2014, 2016, 2018, 2020, and 2022

Personal Information
• Fluent in Portuguese and Spanish
• His parents are immigrants, but he was born and raised in Hanford, California
• His father established a dairy farm in Kings County, growing almonds, corn and wheat
