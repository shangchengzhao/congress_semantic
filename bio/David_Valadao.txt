David Valadao

Educational Background
• Attended the College of the Sequoias in Visalia as a part-time student; did not graduate

Career
• Elected member of the California State Assembly from the 30th District (2010-2012) and U.S. House of Representatives from California from the 21st (2021-2023) and 22nd Districts (2023)
• Current member of the California Milk Advisory Board and Western States Dairy Trade association; appointed Regional Leadership Council chairman for Land O’ Lakes
• Supports caring for farmers, ranchers, and veterans, quality and affordable healthcare for all Americans, immigration reform, and comprehensive education; does not support qualified immunity and gun-control regulation

Personal Information
• Fluent in Portuguese and Spanish
• Parents are immigrants, but he was born and raised in Hanford, California
• Father established a dairy farm in Kings County, growing almonds, corn and wheat
