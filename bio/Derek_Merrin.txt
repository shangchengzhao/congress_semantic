Derek Merrin

Educational Background
• Graduated from Monclova Christian Academy in Ohio
• Attended Owens Community College before earning his Bachelor of Arts degree from University of Toledo
• Master of Public Administration degree from Bowling Green State University

Career
• Elected to Waterville City Council at 19, two years later he was elected mayor for three years
• Leader of the Ohio House Republican Caucus; served eight years in the Ohio legislature
• Chairman of the House Health Committee and the House Ways and Means Committee

Personal Information
• Born in Long Island, New York and moved to Waterville, Ohio when he was thirteen
• Lives in Monclova Township, Ohio
• Bengals fan and real estate investor
