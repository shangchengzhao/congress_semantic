Derek Tran

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science degree in Economics-Finance from Bentley University
• Juris Doctor degree from Glendale University College of Law

Career
• Enlisted in the U.S. army at 18 and was activated for Operations Noble Eagle and Enduring Freedom before receiving an honorable discharge
• Worked as an attorney since 2014 and founded his own law firm, the Tran Firm, in Huntington Beach in 2020
• Has served on the board of the Consumer Attorneys Association of America and was appointed the traffic commissioner for Orange

Personal Information
• Grew up in the San Gabriel Valley as the son of Vietnamese refugees
• Co-owns a pharmacy in Anaheim with his wife
• Lives in the city of Orange with his wife Michelle and their three kids
