Derek Tran

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Economics-Finance from Bentley University
• Juris Doctor degree from Glendale University College of Law

Career
• Worked as an attorney since 2014; founded his own law firm (2020), served on the board of Consumer Attorneys Association of America, and was appointed traffic commissioner for Orange, California
• Enlisted in the U.S. army at 18 years old; activated for Operations Noble Eagle and Enduring Freedom before receiving an honorable discharge

Personal Information
• Grew up in the San Gabriel Valley as the son of Vietnamese refugees
• Co-owns a pharmacy in Anaheim with his wife
• Lives in the city of Orange with his wife Michelle and their three kids
