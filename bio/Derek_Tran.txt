Educational Background
Graduated with a B.S. from Bentley University (Wikipedia)
Earned his Juris Doctor degree from Glendale University College of Law (Wikipedia)

Career
Enlisted in the U.S. army when he was 18 and served for eight years in the U.S. Army Reserve; activated for Operations Noble Eagle and Enduring Freedom before receiving an honorable discharge (Wikipedia)
Tran has worked as an attorney since 2014 and founded his own law firm, the Tran Firm, in 2020 in Huntington Beach; his firm was acquired by Feher Law in 2023 (Wikipedia)
Has served on the board of the Consumer Attorneys Association of America and was appointed the traffic commissioner for Orange (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Tran co-owns a pharmacy in Anaheim with his wife  (Wikipedia)
He lives in the city of Orange with his wife Michelle and their three kids  (Wikipedia)
