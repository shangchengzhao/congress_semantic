Derrick Van Orden

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree from Excelsior University

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Wisconsin’s 3rd District (2023)
• Joined the United States Navy at 18; served for 26 years including as a Navy SEAL Senior Chief for 26 years
• Supports the overturning of Roe v. Wade, rebuilding the economy post-pandemic, improving healthcare and reducing costs, and working on behalf of farmers; does not support forgiveness of federal student loan debt and gun-control legislation

Personal Information
• Co-operated the Butternut Cafe in Northern Wisconsin
• Van Orden lives with his wife Sra jane in Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin
• The couple have four children and eleven grandchildren
