Derrick Van Orden

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science degree from Excelsior University

Career
• Joined the United States Navy at 18, served for 26 years including as a Navy SEAL Senior Chief for 26 years
• Ran for United States House of Representatives in Wisconsin’s 3rd Congressional District in 2022 and won
• Played the role of Senior Chief Otto Miller in 2012 film Act of Valor and roles in Surviving the Wild and Running with the Devil

Personal Information
• Co-operated the Butternut Cafe in Northern Wisconsin
• Van Orden lives with his wife Sra jane in Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin
• The couple have four children and eleven grandchildren
