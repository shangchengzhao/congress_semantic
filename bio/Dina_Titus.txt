Educational Background
Bachelor degree in Political Science (Wikipedia)
Masters degree from the University of Georgia (Wikipedia)
Earned her Ph.D from Florida State University (Wikipedia)

Career
Titus taught in the political science department at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas and retired in 2011 (Wikipedia)
Titus was elected to the Nevada Senate in 1988 where she served for 20 years represented the 7th district (Wikipedia)
Elected to House of Representatives in 2008, she was re-elected in 2010, 2012, 2014, 2016, 2018, 2020, and 2022 (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Titus has been married to her husband Thomas since 1979 (Wikipedia)
Dina enjoys traveling and her travels have taken her all over the world (Titus Campaign Website)
