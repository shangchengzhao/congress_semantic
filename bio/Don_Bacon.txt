Educational Background
Bachelor of Arts degree from Northern Illinois University, interned in Representative Ed Madigan’s Washington D.C. office in 1984 (Wikipedia)
Master of Business Administration from University of Phoenix (Wikipedia)
Master of Arts degree from National War College (Wikipedia)

Career
Joined the Air Force in 1985, specialized in electronic warfare, intelligence, reconnaissance and public affairs, qualified Master Navigator; commander in multiple expeditions (Wikipedia)
Served as a public affairs aide for General David Petraeus and U.S. representative Fortenberry (Wikipedia)
Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2016 representing Nebraska’s 2nd District, reelected in 2018, 2020, and 2022 (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Don and his wife Angie have four children and eight grandchildren (Wikipedia)
He lives with his wife in Papillion Nebraska and is a very family oriented person (Wikipedia)
