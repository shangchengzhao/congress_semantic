Educational Background
Earned his Bachelor of Science degree from the United States Air Force Academy (Wikipedia)
Master of Science degree from Central Michigan University (Wikipedia)
Master of arts in sociology and doctorate in education from East Carolina University (Wikipedia)

Career
Commissioned to the Air Force and served for eight years, rising to the rank of captain; coordinator of Air Force One operations at Joint Base Andrews (Wikipedia)
Taught as an assistant professor of aerospace studies at Air Force ROTC department at East Carolina University (Wikipedia)
Ran for North Carolina State Senate in 2010 and was defeated, ran again in 2012 and won; he was later elected to the United States House of Representatives in 2022 (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Don is married to his wife Yuvonka (Wikipedia)
The couple live in Greene County, North Carolina and have three sons (Wikipedia)
