Donald Davis

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science degree from the United States Air Force Academy
• Master of Science degree from Central Michigan University
• Master of Arts in Sociology and a doctorate in education at East Carolina University

Career
• Served as Mayor of Snow Hill (2001-2008) and as the chair of the Democratic Party for North Carolina’s 1st Congressional District (2008-2020)
• Served in the United States Air Force for eight years; highest rank of Captain
• Ran for North Carolina State Senate in 2012 and won; later elected to the United States House of Representatives in 2022

Personal Information
• He and his wife, Yuvonka, live in Greene County, North Carolina and have three sons
• Presbyterian and a lay minister
• Worked in the tobacco fields and attended church regularly as a young man
