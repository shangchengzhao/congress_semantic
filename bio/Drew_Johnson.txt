Drew Johnson

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Political Science from Belmont University
• Master of Public Policy degree from Pepperdine University
• Koch Fellow at the Institute for Humane Studies and the American Enterprise Institute

Career
• Founded the Tennessee Center for Policy Research (2004) and named the National Director of a group to fight net neutrality rules called Protect Internet Freedom (2016)
• Senior fellow at the Taxpayers Protection Alliance (2011) and senior scholar at National Center for Public Policy Research (2017); worked at the National Taxpayers Union and the American Enterprise Institute
• Supports exposing wasteful spending and ensuring respectful use of taxpayer money, term limits for those in House and Senate, and government transparency; does not support red tape that discourages domestic energy production

Personal Information
• Lived out of his car while he was starting his career
• Married to Sara Reeves Johnon who is a marketing consultant and travel blogger
• The couple moved to Las Vegas in 2015, seeking a friendly environment to start a small business
