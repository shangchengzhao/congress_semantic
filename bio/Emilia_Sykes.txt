Educational Background
Attended Tuskegee University (Wikipedia)
Earned her Bachelor of Arts degree in psychology from Kent State University (Wikipedia)
Got her Juris Doctor and Master of Public Health degree from the University of Florida (Wikipedia)

Career
Worked as a law clerk for the chief judge of the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Northern District of Georgia and later served as an administrative advisor to the Summit County fiscal office (Wikipedia)
Sykes was elected to the Ohio House of Representatives in 2013; she passed legislation to remove sales tax on feminine hygiene products; elected as leader of Democratic caucus in 2019 and doubled bipartisan bills passed in the house (Wikipedia)
She was elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022 representing Ohio’s 13th district (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Emilia’s mother and father, Barbara and Vernon Sykes, were both state representatives who held the same seat for 32 years (Wikipedia)
She is married to her husband Kevin (Wikipedia)
