Emilia Sykes

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Kent State University
• Juris Doctor degree from the University of Florida Levin College of Law
• Master of Public Health from the College of Public Health and Health Professions

Career
• Law clerk for the chief judge of the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in the Northern district of Georgia
• Administrative advisor in the Summit County fiscal office
• Served on the Ohio House of Representatives in the 34th District (2015-2022); became minority leader (2019-2021)

Personal Information
• Married to politician Kevin Boyce
• She is the daughter of state senator Vernon Sykes and former representative Barbara Sykes
• She grew up dancing and competing in gymnastics, making it to the AAU Junior Olympics (vault)
