Eric Sorenson

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science degree in Communications and Meteorology from Northern Illinois University

Career
• Meteorologist at KTRE (1999-2000) and East Texas News Daybreak, chief meteorologist for WREX (2003-2014), and senior meteorologist for WQAD
• Joined the United States House of Representatives in 2022 representing Illinois’s 17th Congressional District
• Fellow of the Society for Environmental Journalists (2018)

Personal Information
• Eric is the first openly gay person to be elected to Congress from Illinois
• He lives with his partner, Shawn, in Moline with two dogs named Petey and Oliver
