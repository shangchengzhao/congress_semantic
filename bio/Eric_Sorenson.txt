Educational Background
Earned his Bachelor of Science degree from Northern Illinois University where he studied meteorology and communications (Wikipedia)

Career
Began his career as a meteorologist at KTRE (ABC affiliate in Lufkin, Texas) for a year before being the meteorologist for East Texas News Daybreak (Wikipedia)
Worked as chief meteorologist for WREX (Rockford’s NBC affiliate) for 11 years before becoming the senior meteorologist for WQAD, the ABC affiliate of Moline, Illinois (Wikipedia)
Joined the United States House of Representatives in 2022 representing Illinois’s 17th congressional district  (Wikipedia)

Personal Information
Eric is the first openly gay person to be elected to Congress from Illinois  (Wikipedia)
He lives with his partner Moline (Wikipedia)
