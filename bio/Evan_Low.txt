Evan Low

Educational Background
• Associate’s degree from De Anza College
• Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from San Jose State University

Career
• Served as Mayor and City Councilmember in Campbell, California (2009-2014)
• Represented the Silicon Valley region (District 26) in the California State Assembly (2014-2024)
• Served as chair of the California Legislative LGBT Caucus (2017-2018; 2021-2022)

Personal Information
• Youngest gay, Asian American mayor in the nation
• Son of Chinese-American optometrist Arthur Low
• Gavin Newsom (at the time San Francisco Mayor) issued a proclamation naming June 5, 2006, “Evan Low Day” in the City and County of San Francisco
