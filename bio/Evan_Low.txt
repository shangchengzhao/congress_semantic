Evan Low

Educational Background
• Associate’s degree from De Anza College
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Political Science from San Jose State University

Career
• Elected member of the California State Assembly to represent Districts 28 (2014-2022) and 26 (2022) and Mayor and City Councilmember in Campbell, California (2009-2014)
• Elected to serve as chair of the California Legislative LGBT Caucus (2017-2018; 2021-2022)
• Supports abortion rights, reducing cost of living and promoting housing affordability, combatting homelessness, and preventing gun violence; he is against political hate from politicians and citizens alike

Personal Information
• Youngest gay, Asian American mayor in the nation
• Son of Chinese-American optometrist Arthur Low
• Gavin Newsom (at the time San Francisco Mayor) issued a proclamation naming June 5, 2006, “Evan Low Day” in the City and County of San Francisco
