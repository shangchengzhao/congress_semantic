Frank Mrvan

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Journalism from Ball State University

Career
• Elected township trustee for North Township, Indiana (2005-2021) and U.S. Representative for Indiana’s 1st Congressional District in the U.S. House of Representatives (2021-2023)
• Worked previously as a licensed mortgage broker and pharmaceutical sales representative
• Supports abortion rights, government regulation of prescription drug costs, and economic interventions as a means of resolving international conflicts; does not support anti-semitism and socialism within America

Personal Information
• Mrvan and his wife, Jane, have two daughters named Genevieve and Scarlett
• His father, Frank E. Mrvan, served in the Indiana Senate (1978-1995 and 1998-2022)
