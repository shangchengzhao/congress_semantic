Gabe Evans

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Government from Patrick Henry College; cum laude graduate
• Masters of Public Policy and Administration at Colorado State University

Career
• Elected member of the Colorado House of Representatives from the 48th District (2023)
• Served on the Virginia Army National Guard (2007-2009) and the Colorado National Guard (2009-2019); concurrently served as lieutenant of the Arvada Police while serving in the Colorado National Guard
• Supports strengthening border security, education reform, school choice, and homeschooling, and reducing cost of living by making essentials affordable; does not support increasing federal funding for affordable housing programs

Personal Information
• Married to Anne Evans, an author and counselor
• He and his wife have two sons and live on their ranch in Fort Lupton
• Grandson of Mexican immigrants
