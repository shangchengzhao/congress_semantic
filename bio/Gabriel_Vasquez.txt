Gabriel Vasquez

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Arts degree in English and Journalism from New Mexico State University

Career
• Business editor for the Las Cruces Bulletin (2008-2011); Executive director of the Las Cruces Hispanic Chamber of Commerce
• Field representative for the U.S. Senator Martin Heinrich and vice president of communications for First Focus on Children
• Served on the Las Cruces City Council (2017-2021)

Personal Information
• First-generation Mexican American, raised in the U.S.-Mexico border region
• Spent much of his time in his grandfather’s TV repair shop in the Chaveña Neighborhood of Ciudad Juarez
