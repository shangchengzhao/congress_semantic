George Logan

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Engineering from Trinity College
• Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Bridgeport

Career
• Elected member of the Connecticut State Senate for the 17th District (2017-2021); previous board member for Griffin Hospital, the Greater Valley Chamber of Commerce, the Central Connecticut Coast YMCA, etc.
• Engineer and executive for the Aquarion Water Company; Director of Environmental Management until 2020
• Supports opportunity in education and specialized education, promoting affordability, and fighting job-killing policies that crush small businesses; does not support a government larger than it already is

Personal Information
• Parents were born and raised in Guatemala and immigrated to the United States as adults
• Fluent in Spanish; self-described Afro-Latino
• Frontman for the Jimi Hendrix tribute band Electric Lady Band USA
