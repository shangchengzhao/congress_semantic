Henry Cuellar

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Science in Foreign Service from the Walsh School of Foreign Service at Georgetown University
• Master of Arts in International Trade from Texas A&M International University
• Juris Doctor (JD) from the University of Texas School of Law, and a Ph.D. from the University of Texas at Austin

Career
• Served in the Texas House of Representatives for 14 years (1987-2001) and (briefly) Texas Secretary of State (2001)
• Adjunct professor of international commercial law at Texas A&M International University
• Opened his own law firm in Laredo (1981)

Personal Information
• One of eight children to migrant farmworkers; first in his family to attend college
• Portrayed George Washington during the annual Washington's Birthday Celebration in Laredo
• Him and his wife, Imelda, have two daughters together
