Hillary Scholten

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Arts degree from Gordon College
• Juris Doctor degree from the University of Maryland Thurgood School of Law

Career
• Attorney adviser for the Board of Immigration Appeals in the U.S. Department of Justice (2013-2017)
• Clerked for the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit
• Elected U.S. Representative for Michigan’s 3rd Congressional District (2022)

Personal Information
• First woman and mother to represent Michigan’s 3rd congressional district
• Her parents raised her in the Christian Reformed Church
• She is married to Jesse Holcomb, a journalism professor at Calvin University
