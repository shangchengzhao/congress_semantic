Janelle Bynum

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Florida A&M University
• Master of Business Administration from the University of Michigan

Career
• Elected member of the Oregon House of Representatives from the 51st District (2017-2023)
• Has also worked as a steering systems engineer at General Motors and assisted her mother-in-law in running a McDonald’s franchise in Clackamas County, Oregon
• Supports addressing housing affordability, ending homelessness, combating climate change, lowering the cost of healthcare, and improving education accessibility; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade

Personal Information
• Her and her husband Mark have four children and live in Happy Valley
• Her and her family-in-law own several McDonald’s franchises in Portland
• She is a lover of all sports, the arts, and good barbeque
