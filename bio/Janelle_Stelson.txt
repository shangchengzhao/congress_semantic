Janelle Stelson

Educational Background
• Bachelor of Arts degree in Politics and Government from the University of Puget Sound

Career
• She has ~40 years of experience in journalism; general assignment reporter at WHTM, reporter for WPLG, anchor for WHP, employee for WITF radio, anchor on PBS, co-anchor for WGAL
• First job was as a speech writer for the Egyptian embassy in Washington, D.C.
• Supports reproductive rights, bringing down the cost of living, promoting high-quality and affordable healthcare, investing in blue collar jobs, and honoring veterans; does not support open borders

Personal Information
• Received several Associated Press awards for her reporting
• Named “Best News Anchor” many times during her time at WGAL by an annual reader poll for Senior News of Lancaster and Dauphin County
• Father was a state trooper and teacher and her mother was a receptionist at the Navy shipyard
