Jared Golden

Educational Background
• Attended the University of Maine at Farmington from 2001-2002
• Bachelor of Arts degree in History and Politics from Bates College

Career
• Worked for the House Democratic Office in the Maine Legislature
• Elected U.S. Representative for Maine’s 2nd Congressional District (2019)
• Member of the Main House of Representatives (2014-2018) before running for federal office

Personal Information
• Has several tattoos from his time in the military, including a Celtic cross on his forearm and a "devil dog" which represents his Marine unit
• Lives in Lewiston with his wife, Isobel, and their two daughters
