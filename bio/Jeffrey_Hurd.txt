Jeffrey Hurd

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Philosophy from the University of Notre Dame
• Juris Doctor degree from the University of Denver
• Master of Laws degree from Columbia University

Career
• Clerked for a federal appeals court judge in the U.S. Court of Appeals for the 10th Circuit and worked for the Grand Junction Area Chamber of Commerce
• Practiced law at Sullivan & Cromwell in New York before starting his own law form; currently operates the Grand Junction office for one of New York’s largest law firms
• Supports federally-issued permits for drilling on public lands, increasing security along the Southern U.S. border, investing in energy independence, and empowering working families; does not support reproductive rights

Personal Information
• He and his wife, Barbora, have five children together and live in Grand Junction
