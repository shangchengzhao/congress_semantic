John Duarte

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Art in Finance from San Diego State University
• MBA from University of the Pacific’s Eberhardt School of Business

Career
• Won the 2022 U.S. House of Representatives California District 13 seat in 2022
• Vice President of Sales at Duarte Trees & Vines (1989), then became the company’s chief executive officer and president (2007)
• Founded Duarte Nursery, the largest permanent crops nursery in the United States

Personal Information
• Lives with his wife, Alexandra, on a pistachio and almond farm outside of Modesto in rural Stanislaus County
• Is an Episcopalian
