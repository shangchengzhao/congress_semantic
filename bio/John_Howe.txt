John Howe

Educational Background
• Bachelors of Arts and Masters of Science Degrees in Criminal Justice Studies at St. Cloud University

Career
• Member of the Minnesota Senate for the 28th district (2011-2013)
• Mayor of Red Wing (city in Minnesota; 2008-2011) and a member of the Goodhue County Social Services Board
• Worked as a corrections officer at the St. Cloud correctional facility and Chairman of Sears Dealer Council

Personal Information
• Has three children with his wife Lisa
• Raised on a farm in Chokio, Minnesota
