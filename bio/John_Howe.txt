John Howe

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Criminal Justice Studies at St. Cloud University
• Masters of Science Degrees in Criminal Justice Studies at St. Cloud University

Career
• Elected member of the Minnesota Senate from the 28th District (2011-2023), mayor of Red Wing, Minnesota (2008-2011) and member of the Goodhue County Social Services Board
• Worked as a corrections officer at the St. Cloud correctional facility and Chairman of Sears Dealer Council
• Supports combating national debt, promoting natural security, returning power to the states, and investing in public education; does not support political divide and animosity between citizens

Personal Information
• Has three children with his wife Lisa
• Raised on a farm in Chokio, Minnesota
