John James

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Law and Systems (Industrial) Engineering from United States Military Academy at West Point
• MBA from the University of Michigan
• MS in Supply Chain Management and Information Systems from Pennsylvania State University

Career
• Represents Michigan’s 10th Congressional District (2022); member of several committees including foreign affairs, transportation, and education
• Served eight years in the Army; multiple tours of duty in Operation Iraqi Freedom and became a Ranger-qualified aviation officer
• Served as President of James Group International (JGI) and CEO of Renaissance Global Logistics

Personal Information
• Married to Elizabeth James since 2012; they have three sons
• Is a nondenominational Christian
• First Black Republican elected to Congress from Michigan
