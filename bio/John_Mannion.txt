John Mannion

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Biology from Binghamton University
• Master of Science in Secondary Science Education from SUNY Oswego

Career
• Elected member of the New York State Senate from the 50th District (2020-2023); was the chairperson of the Committee on Disabilities and member of several committees, such as Children and Families and Education
• Was an AP Biology and Chemistry teacher at a high school for almost 30 years
• Supports transitioning to a greener economy, promoting clean energy, greater funding and employment for the disabled, and gun-control legislation; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade

Personal Information
• Has been married to Jennifer Brady since 1997; they have three children
• His wife has been an Elementary Reading teacher for almost 30 years
• Grandparents moved to New York from Ireland 100 years ago
