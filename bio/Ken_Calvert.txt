Ken Calvert

Educational Background
• Associate of Arts degree in Business from Chaffey Community College
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree from San Diego State University

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from California for Districts 43 (1993-2003), 44 (2003-2013), 42 (2013-2023), and 41 (2023) and chair of the Subcommittee on Defense for the Committee on Appropriations

• Was a small business owner in the restaurant and real estate business after graduating college
