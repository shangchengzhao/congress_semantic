Kevin Coughlin

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree from Bowling Green State University
• Master’s in Public Administration from Bowling Green State University

Career
• Won the seat as the Ohio House of Representatives for the 46th district (1997-2001)
• Appointed as Ohio senator for the 27th District (2001-2010)
• Was Chairman of the Senate Health, Human Services, and Aging Committee when part of senate (2004-2011)

Personal Information
• Has been married to Anne for 30 years, a physical therapist; they have two daughters
• Grew up in Ohio with a single mom before moving in with his grandparents
• Worked while attending college to financially support himself
