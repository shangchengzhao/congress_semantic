Kirsten Engel

Educational Background
• Graduated magna cum laude with a Bachelor’s of Arts from Brown University
• Juris Doctor degree from Northwestern University School of Law

Career
• Clerked for a judge, worked for the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, and served as an assistant attorney general in the Massachusetts Attorney’s General’s Office
• Member of the Arizona House of Representatives and the Arizona Senate for the 10th District (2017-2021)
• Joined the faculty at the University of Arizona's James E. Rogers College of Law where she teaches environmental and administrative law

Personal Information
• Is married to Scott Saleska, a climate scientist; they have one daughter named Helene
• Enjoys Arizona’s hiking trails and jogging on the Rillito on the weekends
