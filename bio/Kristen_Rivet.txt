Kristen Rivet

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in History from Michigan State University
• Master’s in Public Administration and education from the University of Michigan-Flint

Career
• Was a member of the Charter Commission for Bay City, Michigan; subsequently elected to the Bay City Commission to represent the city’s 2nd ward
• Worked in Michigan department of human services, chief of staff for a State Superintendent, and headed other health improvement companies
• Represented Michigan’s 35th district as a senator; was an assistant floor manager, where she served on the several subcommittees, such as community development and education

Personal Information
• Grew up in a small town in Michigan in a working-class household
• She and her siblings were first-generation college students
• Is married to Joseph Rivet, a former politician; they have six children
