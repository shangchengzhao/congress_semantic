Lori Chavez-DeRemer

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Business Administration from California State University, Fresno

Career
• Served as mayor of Happy Valley, Oregon (2011-2019)
• Served as a member of the U.S. House of Representatives for Oregon’s 5th District (2023)
• Has won the Republican primary for the Oregon House of Representatives 51 District elections in 2016, 2018, and 2022

Personal Information
• Married to Shawn, an anesthesiologist; they have two children
• Her and her family all live in Happy Valley, Oregon
• First in her family to graduate from college
