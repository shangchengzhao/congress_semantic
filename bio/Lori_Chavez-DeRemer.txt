Lori Chavez-DeRemer

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Business Administration from California State University, Fresno

Career
• Elected to city council of Happy Valley Oregon (2002), mayor of this same city (2011-2019), and member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Oregon’s 5th District (2023)
• Won the Republican primary for the Oregon House of Representatives 51st District elections in 2016, 2018, and 2022
• Supports ending cancel culture, championing constitutional rights, putting America first, parental choice in education, lower taxes, and balanced budgets; does not support teaching critical race theory

Personal Information
• Married to Shawn, an anesthesiologist; they have two children
• Her and her family all live in Happy Valley, Oregon
• First in her family to graduate from college
