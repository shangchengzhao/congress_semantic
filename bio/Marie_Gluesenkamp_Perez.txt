Marie Gluesenkamp Perez

Educational Background
• Home-schooled in her early years by her mother
• Attended Warren Wilson College; Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Economics after transferring to Reed College

Career
• Co-Chair of the Blue Dog Coalition for Communications (2023)
• Represented Washington’s 3rd District in the U.S. House of Representatives (2023)
• Served on the Underwood Conservation District board (2018) and the Washington State Democratic Party executive committee (2020-2022)

Personal Information
• Met her husband, Dean, while co-managing Reed Bike Co-Op at Reed College
• She and her husband have one child and a dog named Uma Furman
• She and her family live near Stevenson, Washington, in Skamania County
