Mark Robertson

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in finance from Brigham Young University
• Master’s in Business Administration from the City University of Seattle

Career

• Supports increasing border security, prioritizing American citizens, parent choice in education, defending constitutional rights, controlling spending, and cutting inflation; does not support gun-control legislation

Personal Information

• Is the leader of his church
• Volunteered his time to coach Little League, soccer, and basketball
