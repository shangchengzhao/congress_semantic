Mary Peltola

Educational Background
• Studied Elementary Education at the University of Northern Colorado
• Took courses at the University of Alaska Fairbanks, University of Alaska Southeast, and University of Alaska Anchorage

Career
• Member of the Alaska House of Representatives (39th District from 1999-2003); 38th District from 2003-2009)
• Member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Alaska’s at-large district (2022-2023)
• Co-Chair of the Blue Dog Coalition for Policy (2023)

Personal Information
• She has 4 biological children and 3 stepchildren
• Orthodox Christian and belongs to the Orthodox Church in America
• Alaska Native
