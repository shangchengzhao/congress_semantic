Mayra Flores

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree in Applied Science from South Texas College

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Texas’ 34th District (2022-2023), serving on the Committee on Homeland Security and Committee on Agriculture
• Previously worked as a respiratory therapist and chair of Hispanic outreach for the Hidalgo County Republican Party
• Supports religious freedom, school choice, abortion bans, securing the border, caring for U.S. veterans, and protecting social security; does not support same-sex marriage, politics in education, and renewable energy options

Personal Information
• First female Mexican-born member of the House
• Was previously married to a U.S. Border Patrol agent
• Worked in cotton fields in Texas alongside her parents while growing up to earn extra money
