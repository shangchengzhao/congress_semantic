Michael Lawler

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Accounting and Finance from Manhattan College
• Valedictorian of his graduating class

Career
• Member of the New York State Assembly from the 97th District (2021-2022)
• Served as Rob Astorino’s Campaign Manager (2014), his assistant as County Executive, and Republican convention delegate for Donald Trump (2016)
• Co-founded the political communications firm Checkmate Strategies (2018)

Personal Information
• Described Michael Jackson as his “musical hero” and managed to get into the courtroom of Jackson’s trial with the help of a biographer
• Lives in Pearl River, New York with his wife and their daughters
• Life long New York resident
