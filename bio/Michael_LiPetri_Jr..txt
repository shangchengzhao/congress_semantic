Michael LiPetri Jr.

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Political Science and Government from the University at Albany
• Juris Doctor degree from the Albany Law School of Union University

Career
• Elected member of the New York State Assembly from the 9th District (2019-2021)
• Was an associate attorney at Rivkin Radler, LLP and assistant corporation counsel for the New York City Law Department
• Supports increased penalties for sex crimes involving a minor, banning the use of products containing carcinogen, and tax deductible supplies for college students; did not support forcing nursing homes to accept COVID-19 positive patients

Personal Information
• Worked as a talk show host previously
• His grandfather, Angelo LiPetri, was a pitcher for the Phillies during the 1950s
