Michelle Steel

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Business from Pepperdine University
• Master’s in Business Administration from the University of Southern California
• Was educated in South Korea, Japan, and the United States

Career
• Member of the California State Board of Equalization from the 3rd District (2007-2015)
• Member of the Orange County Board of Supervisors (2015-2021) and chair of the same board (2020-2021)
• Member of the U.S. House of Representatives from California (45th District: 2023; 48th District: 2021-2023)

Personal Information
• Is trilingual; can speak Korean, Japanese, and English
• Married her husband Shawn in 1981; they have two daughters and all live in Seal Beach, California
• Emigrated from South Korea with her mom and two younger sisters
