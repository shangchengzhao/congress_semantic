Mike Erickson

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s Degree in Business from Portland State University

Career
• Founder and President of AFMS Logistics Management Group, which helps companies negotiate competitive shipping contracts
• This company made Inc. magazine’s list of the 500 fastest growing companies in the United States twice (20024, 2005)
• Republican nominee for the United States House of Representatives seat in Oregon’s 5th Congressional District (2006)

Personal Information
• Placekicker and punter on Portland State’s football team (1985-1988)
• Has a wife and two children; they live in Clackamas County in Oregon
