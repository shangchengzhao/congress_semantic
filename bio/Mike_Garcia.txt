Mike Garcia

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Political Science from the United States Naval Academy
• Master’s of Arts in National Security Policy from Georgetown University (graduated top 3% of class)

Career
• Served in the U.S. Navy and reached the rank Lieutenant Commander; active duty from 1998-2009 and on reserve from 2009-2012
• Member of the U.S. House representing the 25th District (2020-2023) and 27th District (2023)
• Worked as a business development manager at Raytheon Intelligence, Information and Services

Personal Information
• He married his wife Rebecca, an interior designer; they have two sons
• He is a first-generation Mexican-American
