Nicholas J. LaLota

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree from the United States Naval Academy
• Cum Laude graduate from Hofstra University’s Zarb School of Business with Master of Business Administration
• Juris Doctor degree from Hofstra University’s Maurice A. Deane School of Law

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from New York’s 1st District (2023); Served in the U.S. Navy for eight years, being deployed three times overseas and reaching the rank of Lieutenant
• Has served in different leadership roles; appointed to Amityville Board of Trustees, Amityville Budget Officer, Chairman of the Committee to the Police Department, Chief of Staff to Suffolk County Legislature, etc.
• Supports Israel and Ukraine, maintaining border security, term limits for those in power, cutting excess spending, and protecting social security, healthcare, and veterans; does not support sanctuary city policies

Personal Information
• His brother, Dan, is a retired Marine
• His father served as a Nassau County Police officer, his grandfathers served in the NYPD, and his stepfather served in the Navy
• Married his highschool sweetheart Kaylie, a high school teacher; they have three children
