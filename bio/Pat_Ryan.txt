Pat Ryan

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in International Politics from the U.S. Military Academy
• Master of Arts degree in Security Studies from Georgetown University

Career
• 2nd County Executive of Ulster County (2019-2022), Representative for New York’s 19th Congressional District (2022-2023), and Representative for New York’s 18th Congressional District (2023)
• U.S. Army military intelligence officer (2004-2009); attained the rank of captain and was awarded two Bronze Star Medals, an Army Commendation Model, and Army Achievement Medal
• Has worked at various technology companies, including as a deputy director of Berico Technologies (2009-2011), co-founded Praescient Analytics (2011), and senior vice president of Dataminr (2015-2017)

Personal Information
• Is a Roman Catholic
• Fifth-generation Ulster County native
• Lives in Gardiner, Maine with his wife Rebecca and their two children
