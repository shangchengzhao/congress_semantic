Pat Ryan

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in International Politics from the U.S. Military Academy
• Master of Arts degree in Security Studies from Georgetown University

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from New York (2022-2023) and 2nd County Executive of Ulster County (2019-2022); U.S. Army military intelligence officer, attaining the rank captain and many awards
• Has worked at various technology companies, including as a deputy director of Berico Technologies (2009-2011), co-founded Praescient Analytics (2011), and senior vice president of Dataminr (2015-2017)
• Supports increasing federal funding for social safety net programs, affordable housing programs, and the development of renewable energy; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade

Personal Information
• Is a Roman Catholic
• Fifth-generation Ulster County native
• Lives in Gardiner, Maine with his wife Rebecca and their two children
