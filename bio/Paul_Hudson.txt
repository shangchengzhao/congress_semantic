Paul Hudson

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s degree from Cornell University
• Juris Doctor degree from Georgetown University Law Center

Career
• Has worked as an attorney, chair of Sixth Circuit Advisory Committee on Rules, member and diversity chair of the American Bar Association Appellate Practice Committee, and appellate practice and litigation section council for the State Bar of Michigan
• Was the editor-in-chief for the State Bar of Michigan Litigation Newsletter
• Served on the board of directors for the Duffy Daugherty Memorial Award and Goodwill Industries of Southwest Michigan

Personal Information
• His wife is a college professor and Aerospace Engineering PhD; together they have two children
• Has run three marathons, a dozen half-marathons, two Tough Mudders, and a handful of triathlons
• His grandfather is Michigan State football coach Cuffy Daugherty
