Randell Niemeyer

Educational Background
• Attended Indiana State University

Career
• Elected member and president of the Cedar Lake Town Council (12 years)
• Fourth generation milk hauler and owner of the Niemeyer Milk Transfer, Inc. (2016); secured his commercial driver’s license at 18 years old and logged over 3 million miles in his 28 years of being an 18-wheel truck driver
• Supports combating inflation for working and middle-class families, securing rants and expanding local law enforcement, and energy independence; does not support a right to abortion

Personal Information
• Great-grandfather started hauling milk in 1916 to earn extra money from his dairy farm
• Played varsity basketball and baseball, was in the choir, thespian society, and student government at the same time in high school
• His father, daughter, and son graduated from the same high school he attended
