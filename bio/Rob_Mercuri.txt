Rob Mercuri

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science from the U.S. Military Academy
• Master of Business Administration from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst

Career
• Member of the U.S. House representing Pennsylvania’s 28th District (2021-2023)
• Served in the U.S. Army (2000-2009); reached the rank of captain
• Has worked at two accounting firms in New York City, served as a senior vice president and a financial risk manager with PNC, and owns a pack-and-ship business

Personal Information
• He and his wife, Kelsey, own a UPS store location in Wexford, Pennsylvania
• He and his wife have three school-aged children
• Active in his faith community
