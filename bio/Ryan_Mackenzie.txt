Ryan Mackenzie

Educational Background
• Graduated with honors; Bachelor’s of Science degree in Finance and International Business from New York University
• Master of Business Administration degree from the Harvard Business School

Career
• Member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives (134th District: 2012-2022; 187th District: 2023)
• Served as the Republican chair of the Labor and Industry Committee and co-chair of the International Relations Caucus (2023); served in six other positions across six different committees in the past
• Served on the executive council of the Lehigh Career & Technical Institute, the board of the Greater Lehigh Valley Chamber of Commerce, and the boards of other non-profit organizations that help seniors and those with special needs

Personal Information
• Nine generations ago, his first ancestor settled and fought in the northampton County Militia during the Revolutionary War
• His mother is Milou Mackenzie, a Lehigh Valley-area Republican state representative for the 131st District
• Married to his wife Chloe; they have a newborn son and a rescue dog
