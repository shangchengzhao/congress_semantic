Ryan Zinke

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Geology from the University of Oregon
• Masters of Business Administration degree from National University
• Master of Science degree in Global Leadership from the University of San Diego

Career
• Served in the Montana Senate (2009-2013), U.S. representative for the at-large Congressional District (2015-2017), and as the 52nd U.S. Secretary of the Interior under President Donald Trump (2017-2019)
• Served in the U.S. Navy (1986-2008); reached the rank of Commander and was part of the Navy SEALs unit
• Formed a property management and business development consulting company called Continental Divide International (2005) and a consulting company called On Point Montana (2009).

Personal Information
• Married his wife Lolita in 1992; they have two children together
• Star athlete in high school and accepted a football scholarship to the University of Oregon
• Splits time among Washington D.C., Whitefish in Montana, and Santa Barbara in California
