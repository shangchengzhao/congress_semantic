Scott Perry

Educational Background
• Associates degree from Harrisburg Area Community College
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Business Administration and Management from Pennsylvania State University
• Master of Science degree in Strategic Planning from the U.S. Army War College

Career
• Elected Member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Pennsylvania (2013-2023), member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives from the 92nd District (2007-2012), and Chair of the House Freedom Caucus (2022-2023)
• Pennsylvania Army National Guard (1980-2019); eventually reached the rank of brigadier general (2015) and was selected as assistant adjutant general at the Pennsylvania National Guard’s Joint Force (2016)
• Supports strengthening the economy and lowering taxes, preserving healthcare and social security, slashing inflation and reducing cost of living and investing in infrastructure; does not support a federal right to abortion

Personal Information
• Began working at 13 years old, picking fruit on a farm before working as a mechanic, dock worker, draftsman, and license insurance agent
• Was raised in a simple home that initially had no electricity and plumbing, pumping water from a well and cutting firewood with his brother
• Married to his wife Perry; they have two children
