Susan Wild

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree from American University
• Juris Doctor degree from George Washington University

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Pennsylvania (2018-2023), Chair of the House Ethics Committee (2022-2023), Ranking Member of the House Ethics Committee, and solicitor of Allentown (2015)
• Was co-chair of the New Democrat Coalition Climate Change Task Force, vice chair of a caucus and subcommittee, and partner at a law firm (1999)
• Supports protecting social security and healthcare, defending the environment, promoting quality jobs and economic prosperity, and addressing the opioid epidemic; does not support anti-LGBTQ discrimination

Personal Information
• Was the first woman appointed solicitor of Allentown, Pennsylvania
• Daughter of Norman Leith (member of the U.S. Air Force) and Susan Ellis (a journalist)
