Susan Wild

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree from American University
• Juris Doctor degree from George Washington University

Career
• U.S. Representative for Pennsylvania’s 7th Congressional District (2018-2023) and served the last two months of 2018 as the member for Pennsylvania’s 15th Congressional District after the former Representative resigned
• Co-chaired the New Democrat Coalition Climate Change Task Force
• Was vice chair of both the Congressional Labor and Working Families Caucus and the Subcommittee on Africa, Global Health, Global Human Rights, and International Organizations

Personal Information
• Was the first woman appointed solicitor of Allentown, Pennsylvania
• Daughter of Norman Leith (member of the U.S. Air Force) and Susan Ellis (a journalist)
