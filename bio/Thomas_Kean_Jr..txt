Thomas Kean Jr.

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Art degree from Dartmouth College
• Master of Arts in Law and Diplomacy from the Fletcher School at Tufts University

Career
• Aide to Congressman Bob Franke and social assistant at the Environmental Protection Agency during the H.W. Bush Administration
• Member of the New Jersey General Assembly (2001-2003); represented New Jersey’s 21st Legislative District in the New Jersey senate (2003-2022) and served as minority leader (2008-2022)
• Previously a firefighter, emergency medical technician, and vice president of a fire department

Personal Information
• Married to his wife Rhonda; they have two children together and live in Westfield, New Jersey
