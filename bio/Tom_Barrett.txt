Tom Barrett

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Political Science from Western Michigan University

Career
• Served in the Michigan Senate (2019-2023) and Michigan House of Representatives (2015-2019)
• Worked as an analyst for the Michigan Department of the Treasury
• Served for 21 years in the Army and is a veteran of Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation Iraqi Freedom; reached the rank of Chief Warrant Officer 2 in the Michigan Army National Guard

Personal Information
• Great-grandfather represented Michigan’s 14th Congressional District as a Democrat (1935-1947; 1949-1961)
• Married to his Wife Ashley; they have four children
• Is a Catholic
