Tom Suozzi

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree in Accounting from Boston College
• Juris Doctor degree from Fordham University School of Law

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. Representatives from New York’s 3rd District (2017-2023)
• Elected mayor of Glen Cove (1994-2001) and county executive of Nassau County on Long Island (2002-2009)
• Supports “common-sense” gun violence prevention like universal background checks, legal and accessible abortions, and combating climate change through “bold solutions”; does not support the overturning of Roe v. Wade

Personal Information
• Father was born in Italy and his mother was born in Queens making him a first generation American
• Raised his three children with his wife named Helene in Glen Cove, New York where he too grew up
