Educational Background
Dropped out of Roman Catholic School in Corpus Christi during his junior year but earned his high school equivalency (GED) certificate later (Wikipedia)
Attended Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University on the Corpus Christi Naval Air Station and got his Bachelor of Science degree in aviation business administration (Wikipedia)

Career:
Received his Juris Doctor degree from the Texas Wesleyan University School of Law (Wikipedia)
Career
Intern for congressman Solomon P. Ortiz during law school (Wikipedia)
Established his own law practice a year after getting his JD (Wikipedia)

Personal information:
Ran for the United States House of Representatives in 206 and won, he was re-elected in 2018, 2020, and 2022 (Wikipedia)
Personal Information
Vincente is married to his wife Lorena who is a former teacher and school administrator (Wikipedia)
The couple lives in McAllen, Texas (Wikipedia)
