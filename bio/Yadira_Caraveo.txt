Yadira Caraveo

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Science degree from Regis University
• Doctor of Medicine degree from the University of Colorado School of Medicine

Career
• Elected member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Colorado’s 8th District (2023) and Colorado House of Representatives (2019-2023); U.S. Representative while practicing as a pediatrician
• Completed a residency in pediatrics at the University of New Mexico Health Sciences Center; got involved with the Committee of Interns and Residents union for resident physicians
• Supports expanding funding for preschool, ensuring access to paid family and sick leave, and limiting toxic pollution endangering the health of children and the planet; does not support getting rid of the American Dream

Personal Information
• Born to undocumented Mexican parents who were granted amnesty under the Immigration Reform and Control Act of 1986
• Was Colorado’s first Latina member of Congress
• Is a Roman Catholic
