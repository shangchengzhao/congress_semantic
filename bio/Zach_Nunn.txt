Zach Nunn

Educational Background
• Bachelor’s of Arts degree in Political Science and International Relations from Drake University
• Master of Science degree in Military Operational Art and Science from the Air Command and Staff College at Air University
• Master of Studies in International Security from Selwyn College, Cambridge

Career
• Elected member of the Iowa House of Representatives for the 30th District (2015-2019), Iowa Senate for the 15th District (2019-2023), and U.S. Representative for Iowa’s 3rd Congressional District (2023)
• Member of United States Air Force and later the Iowa National Guard (2004); held the rank of lieutenant colonel and commander of an intelligence group (2021) before being promoted to the rank of colonel
• Supports the decision to overturn Roe v. Wade, requiring ID to vote at the polls, increasing defense spending, and increasing security along the southern US border; does not generally support gun-control legislation

Personal Information
• Married to his wife Kelly; they have six children, two of which were adopted from the foster care system
